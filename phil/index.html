About Mosaic

We believe that financing for home improvements should be simple and worry-free for both contractors and homeowners. That’s why we work with leading solar and home improvement companies across the nation to make financing options available for solar energy systems, batteries, energy-efficient home improvements, and more.

Contractors get user-friendly, professional tools that help them close deals faster, and homeowners get flexible financing options for all types of budgets to use on a wide range of home improvement projects.
