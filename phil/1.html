Definition of mosaic

(Entry 1 of 4) 1 : a surface decoration made by inlaying small pieces of variously colored material to form pictures or patterns also : the process of making it 2 : a picture or design made in mosaic 3 : something resembling a mosaic a mosaic of visions and daydreams and memories Lawrence Shainberg 4 : an organism or one of its parts composed of cells of more than one genotype : chimera sense 3 5 : any of numerous virus diseases of plants characterized by diffuse light and dark green or yellow and green mottling of the foliage — compare tobacco mosaic virus 6 : a composite map made of photographs taken by an aircraft or spacecraft 7 : the part of a television camera tube consisting of many minute photoelectric particles that convert light to an electric charge
